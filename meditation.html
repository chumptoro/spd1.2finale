We oftern harshly judge ourselves over the course of our work day. Maybe our work wasn't up to par. Maybe we wish we could handle a situation better. Maybe we feel we haven't been productive enough. A short meditation allows us to observe our mind and realize: it is okay. We have our challenges. For now, we will just breath in and our. Slowly. Surely.
Benefit 2: Anchor Your Mind
Sometimes we are just going through the motion. We may be doing a task, but it feels as though the task is running us. It could be that the task is challenging and we are lost in it. It could be that the task is boring and we're grinding through. Meditation allows us to re-position ourselves. It wakes us up and give us the reset button so we can dive back in with a sense of purpose and control
