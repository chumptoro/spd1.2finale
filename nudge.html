Nudge

Nudge 101
Nudge is an optional setting. When on, it will direct you away from sites you rely on to destress during work but end up spending too much time on them, such as social media and games.
Nudge is not a site-blocking feature
Nudge is undistruptive. It doesn't block the sites you visit. Instead, it will create calming animation designed to relax your eyes and help you nuge yourself to start a mini meditation or return to work
